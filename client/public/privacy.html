Privacy Policy

We keep things simple and transparent.

What We Collect

  • IP address: Used transiently to determine approximate location and nearest test server; not stored long-term.
  • Test metrics: Download speed, upload speed, ping, jitter. Aggregated to improve accuracy; not tied to personally identifiable information.
  • Browser hints: Connection type (navigator.connection.effectiveType) when available.

Data Usage

Large amounts of data may be transferred (hundreds of MB on fast connections) to assess throughput. If you are on a metered or mobile data plan, consider skipping or limiting tests.

Retention

Metrics are kept only in-memory for your current session and discarded afterward unless explicitly opted-in for analytics. If platform analytics (e.g., Vercel) are enabled, they receive standard web request logs.

Third Parties

Public CDN endpoints may be used for download tests (e.g., Hetzner, Cloudflare). Those providers may log standard access details (IP, user agent).

Your Choices

  • Close the page to stop any ongoing transfer.
  • Disable CDN mode by removing the VITE_CDN_MODE environment variable in future builds.
